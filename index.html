Who I Am

🌟 My Story

I am a software development student specializing in building clean, modern web applications. I focus on turning complex problems into intuitive digital experiences with high-quality CSS aesthetics.

My journey into development didn’t start with knowing everything—it started with curiosity. I wanted to understand how websites are built, how buttons work, and how ideas turn into something you can actually see and use. At the beginning, things were not easy. There were errors, confusion, and moments where nothing worked. But instead of stopping, I kept going.

With time, I started to enjoy the challenge. Every small project I built gave me confidence. I learned that coding is not just about writing lines of code—it’s about thinking, solving problems, and being creative.

At the same time, basketball has always been part of my life. On the court, I learned discipline, teamwork, and how to stay focused even when things get tough. That same mindset helps me as a developer. Whether I’m debugging code or learning something new, I approach it the same way I approach a game—keep practicing, stay patient, and never give up.
